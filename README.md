# AxioFlow Business — Privacy Policy

**Last Updated: March 5, 2026**

AxioFlow Business ("App," "we," "us," or "our") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information we collect, how we use it, and your choices regarding your data.

---

## 1. Information We Collect

### Data You Create in the App

- Business names and logos
- Transactions (income, expenses, custom types)
- Categories, customers, orders, and notes
- Recurring transaction settings

**All of this data is stored locally on your device.** We do not transmit or store your business or transaction data on any external server.

### iCloud Sync (Pro Users Only)

If you subscribe to AxioFlow Pro and enable iCloud Sync, your data is synced across your Apple devices using **Apple iCloud**. This data is governed by [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/). We do not have access to your iCloud data.

### Automatically Collected Information

- **Advertising Data:** The free tier displays ads via Google AdMob. AdMob may collect device identifiers, IP address, and usage data for personalized or contextual advertising. See [Google's Privacy Policy](https://policies.google.com/privacy).
- **Purchase Data:** When you subscribe to AxioFlow Pro, your payment is processed entirely by Apple through In-App Purchase. We do not collect or store your payment information (credit card, Apple ID, etc.). See [Apple's Privacy Policy](https://www.apple.com/legal/privacy/).
- **Crash & Diagnostic Data:** We may use Apple's built-in crash reporting. No personally identifiable information is collected through this.

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide and maintain the App's features
- To process your subscription via Apple In-App Purchase
- To display advertisements in the free tier
- To sync your data across devices via iCloud (if enabled)
- To respond to feedback or support inquiries you send us

## 3. Data Sharing

We do **not** sell, trade, or share your personal data with third parties, except as described below:

| Service | Purpose | Data Shared |
|---------|---------|-------------|
| Apple (In-App Purchase) | Subscription billing | Payment handled by Apple |
| Apple (iCloud) | Data sync across devices | Your app data (encrypted by Apple) |
| Google AdMob | Advertising (free tier) | Device identifiers, usage data |

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- All app data is stored **locally on your device** using an on-device database.
- iCloud-synced data is encrypted and managed by Apple.
- We do not operate any servers that store your data.
- We do not have access to your financial or transaction data.

## 5. Children's Privacy

The App is not directed to children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If you believe a child has provided us with personal data, please contact us so we can delete it.

## 6. Your Rights & Choices

- **Delete your data:** You can delete any business, transaction, or all app data from within the App at any time.
- **Disable iCloud Sync:** You can turn off iCloud Sync in the App's settings.
- **Ad Tracking:** You can limit ad tracking in your device settings (Settings > Privacy & Security > Tracking on iOS).
- **Uninstall:** Removing the App deletes all locally stored data.

## 7. Third-Party Links

The App may contain links to external websites or services. We are not responsible for the privacy practices of those third parties.

## 8. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top. Continued use of the App after changes constitutes acceptance of the revised policy.
